ABOUT ME

My interest in the cinematographic processes has led me, during my 20 years in the profession, to develop my editing, camera and photography skills, learning how to use different types of editing software and cameras. I made several video clips, live concerts, tv shows and fiction storytelling.

My curiosity and desire to tell my own stories also made me a scriptwriter and director. As a filmmaker, documentary storytelling is what motivates me the most.

Anyway, I take every project as a new challenge, and I like my work to be as perfect as possible according to the tools and time available for each project.
